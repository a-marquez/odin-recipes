Deviled Eggs

About the dish

Making for delightful appetizers or sides, deviled eggs boast a rich and creamy flavor that can be highly customized to one's flavor preferences.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 to 2 dashes Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Â¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon snipped fresh chives
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Paprika, for garnish
  • Whole fresh chives, for garnish

Steps

  1. Thoroughly rinse eggs in warm water. Place in a small saucepan.
  2. Cover the eggs with cold water, place pan on medium-high heat, bring to a boil.
  3. Turn off and remove from heat, cover the saucepan and allow the eggs to sit for 10-12 minutes.
  4. Drain water, rinse eggs under running cold water, and peel. Allow eggs to cool in the refrigerator with a loose covering for 15 minutes.
  5. Cut eggs in halves along the lengths and collect the yolks. Gather yolks in a small bowl to mash with a fork.
  6. Add mustard, salt, pepper, Tobasco, and chives. Then stir in mayonnaise.
  7. Fill egg white halves with approximately 1.5 tablespoons of the yolk cream and add a light sprinkling of paprika atop.
  8. Finally garnish with chives.
